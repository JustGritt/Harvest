# Harvest

> This is an attempt to create a simple incremental game. You plant crops, harvest them, and try to accumulate as much currency as possible. There is no end goal, just keep playing and see how much you can get before you get bored.

## How to Play

1. Plant crops by clicking the "Plant" button.
2. Harvest crops by clicking the "Harvest" button.
3. Use the currency you get from harvesting to buy upgrades.
4. Repeat steps 1-3.

## Upgrades

- **Seed planter**: Automatically plants seeds for you.
- **Farmer**: Helps you harvest plants when ready.
- **Sprinkler**: Waters your plants for you, making them grow faster.
- **Fertilizer**: Increases the amount of currency you get from harvesting.
